Conclusion : 
The proposed platform represents a significant advancement over existing diagnostic tools by fusing the 
analytical power of AI with the contextual richness of global public health data. It moves beyond a simple, 
isolated classification to offer a multi-dimensional view of brain tumors. The intended outcome is a user-
friendly web application that empowers individuals with contextualised knowledge, aids researchers in 
identifying new patterns, and provides public health officials with a novel lens for understanding the 
prevalence of brain tumors worldwide.
